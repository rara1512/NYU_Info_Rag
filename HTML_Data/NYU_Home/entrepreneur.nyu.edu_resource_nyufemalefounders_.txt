The NYU Entrepreneurial Institute trains and supports entrepreneurs as part of a new generation of business leaders committed to closing the gender gap in entrepreneurship. Our NYU Female Founders initiative offers monthly “lunch & learn” events, Female Founders Forums, and resources to help founders at NYU and beyond grow or scale their startups.
