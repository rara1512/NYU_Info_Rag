Data privacy notice for alumni and friends of NYU in the EU
NYU Digital Privacy Statement

Email Subscription Opt-Out
The site nyu.edu/alumni/address-change provides you with the opportunity to choose to receive email communications about this site and the groups you are registered with. In all email communications that you receive from this community, except for confirmation emails for event registrations, purchases, donations or other payments, you will be provided an unsubscribe option where you can opt out of the particular type of email communication. You can also modify the emails that you receive at any time by going to your account profile and changing your opt-in status there.
